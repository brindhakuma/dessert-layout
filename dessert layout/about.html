"The dessert house" would be a dedicated establishment for sweet foods and indulgent range of
offering that include brownies,cakes,dessert,chocolates,breadds and savouries.

with stories in over 10 cities.

Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate and this journeycontinues as we open
the dessert house patisseries across the country.
